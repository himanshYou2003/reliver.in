Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reliver?

Reliver is a platform dedicated to providing support for individuals facing day-to-day emotional challenges, stress, and anxiety. We connect users anonymously with compassionate listeners who offer non-judgmental listening and guidance.

How does Reliver work?

Users can access our platform and anonymously share their concerns, stressors, and anxieties with compassionate listeners. These individuals provide empathetic support and suggest coping strategies based on their experience and understanding.

Are the conversations on Reliver confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a top priority for us. All interactions between users and compassionate listeners are anonymous and securely encrypted to protect your privacy.

What types of issues can I discuss on Reliver?

You can discuss any day-to-day emotional challenges, stressors, or anxieties you may be facing. Whether it's relationship issues, financial concerns, insecurities, or any other personal struggle, our platform provides a safe space for you to open up and seek support.

How can I access immediate support on Reliver?

Our platform offers immediate support through our AI companion, which provides curated activities to help you calm down and manage stress in real-time, anywhere, anytime.

How do compassionate listeners benefit from Reliver?

Compassionate listeners gain fulfillment by offering support and guidance to those in need. They provide a listening ear and empathy, helping individuals navigate their emotional challenges with care and understanding.
